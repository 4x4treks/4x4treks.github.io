Ride Along as a Passenger in the Lead Vehicle 

Incredible Benefits:
• You can literally turn up with your clothes and we're good to go!
• Exclusive up-front position - see the wildlife and hear first-hand from your guide
• 2 seats only per tour - plenty of space & time in the front seat
• Great for solo travellers or couples
• Save on vehicle setup or hire costs
• Reduced travel time - fly to the starting point, and fly out at the end
• We supply all the camping gear - comfortable stretcher tents with insulated mattress, chairs, cooking gear, cutlery etc
• You have your own fridge & storage - we'll assist you to buy your food pre-tour if necessary (food extra)
• Cook for yourself - avoid dietary issues
• All inclusive arrangements - from landing to leaving, we've taken care of all the arrangements

A surprising fact is that in Australia, tag along tour guides are not required to be accredited. Therefore, we think it’s important you know that Tony is Savannah Guides accredited, EcoGuide Australia accredited, and Tour Guides Australia accredited (these all require an accreditation process, not just a paid membership).

Tony also holds a Cert IV in Training and Assessment in 4WD Instruction, a Degree in Tourism and a Masters Degree in Wildlife Management. In addition to this, our business is the only tag along tour operator in Australia approved under the Savannah Guides Enterprise accreditation scheme.

What does all this mean to you? It means that when you come along on one of our tours, you are in for the best possible experience, and that your safety is our paramount concern.

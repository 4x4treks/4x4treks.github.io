21 Day Ultimate Africa -
 Namibian Desert, Botswana Game Parks, Victoria Falls 4X4 Trek *June 2023



*Tours dependant on Covid restrictions - deposits refunded or carried over for 12 months if unable to travel. 

This tour takes you from the remote and sparsely populated Namib Desert, to the rich wildlife habitat of the Okavango Delta and ends at the stunning Victoria Falls. 

Travel through this diverse landscape with the assurance of a tag-along group tour. Experienced guides and a local mechanic/interpreter make venturing into this incredible location easy. The wildlife, landscapes and remoteness of this unique part of Africa will have you talking about this trip for a lifetime.

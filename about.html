About Us

AU4X4TREKS takes you to the ultimate off-road and remote locations - places many people would never go by themselves. Our carefully planned tours are designed to let you safely experience incredible 4X4 destinations with like-minded companions. No experience necessary. Just a sense of adventure.


Meet Tony Davys,
AU4X4TREKS

Tony is a professional 4WD instructor (Rockhampton 4WD Training) and remote area tour guide, having been involved in the 4WD industry for the last 18 years, and guiding in Australia for 8 years. With the qualifications of Masters Degree in Wildlife Management, Degree in Tourism and Certificates in 4WD Training, he is also a self-confessed "wildlife fanatic".

Tony is an avid outdoorsman, keen photographer and fisherman and very experienced in the backcountry, learning how to four wheel drive in the rugged mountains of New Zealand’s South Island.

Tony has visited many parts of Australia in the course of his work, safely leading groups of customers in their own vehicles to remote and iconic locations such as the Kimberley, Arnhem Land, Cape York, Flinders Ranges, Central Australia, the Canning Stock Route and the Victorian High Country.

He has had a varied career which has added to his diverse knowledge base. Working for the NZ Department of Conservation deep in the bush on the West Coast of the South Island straight out of Uni was both rewarding and physically challenging. Catching and radio-collaring stoats (like a weasel) and helping to protect vulnerable Kiwi populations was a real highlight of his time on "The Coast".

He then went on to work for (what is now) the Ministry for Primary Industries as a Quarantine Officer (Biosecurity) at Auckland International Airport. The 8 years he spent on the front line dealing with passengers and biosecurity threats taught him a lot about human nature and how to deal with all kinds of people from all over the World. He was also regularly featured on the NZ "Border Patrol" TV series and has some great stories to tell. You can see Tony in his (much) younger days here: [YouTube Link to Border Patrol  45 seconds in].

After moving back to the South Island with his wife, Tony took over an existing 4WD Hire business "Overland 4WD Rentals Ltd". Soon after, the twins came along, and 4 years  after buying the business in 2010 the Christchurch earthquakes hit and pretty much destroyed the business. It wasn't all doom a gloom though. Tony got to meet the most amazing people from all over the World, some becoming life-long friends.  

Due to his business contacts and experience, Tony was offered a job in Australia as a tour guide and 4WD trainer with Vic at Great Divide Tours. The family jumped at the chance (and jumped the ditch) in January 2015 as Christchurch and the tourism industry had never recovered from the earthquakes. Tony quickly fell in love with the wide open spaces and vast landscapes of the Australian Outback.

Due to the urge to run his own business again, Tony moved the family to Rockhampton, QLD in January 2019 and started AU4X4Treks and Rockhampton 4WD Training.

What is a tag-along tour & why travel with our tag-along group?

SAFE AND HASSLE FREE - LIKE MINDED COMPANIONS
We provide a safe and hassle-free way for people to travel to remote locations. Our carefully planned tours are designed to let you safely experience incredible 4X4 locations with like-minded and enthusiastic companions in places most people would never go by themselves.

FOLLOW THE LEADER - NO EXPERIENCE NECESSARY
Very simply put, you follow the tour leader in your own or a hired 4X4 vehicle. And you don't need to have any off-road driving experience because our professional tour guides provide instruction and support throughout each tour to help clients successfully and safely tackle challenging terrain in isolated situations.

LIFE-LONG SKILLS
You will come away from a tour with us with a real sense of achievement and 4X4 skills to last you a lifetime.

LIMITED NUMBERS - VARIED ITINERARIES
We use a network of travel experts around the World to provide a seamless experience for our clients. Up to 8 clients' vehicles come on our tag-along tours which are a mix of self, part and fully catered and accommodated or camping, depending on the location.

EXPERIENCED GUIDES - LOCAL ASSISTANCE
Our guides are experienced 4X4 tour leaders from Australia and New Zealand. On our far-flung International treks, we take a local mechanic who also acts as our interpreter to help us along the way.

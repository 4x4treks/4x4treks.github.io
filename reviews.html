5-Star Google Reviews from our NZ 2024 Tour

"Just returned from the first New Zealand tour with Aus 4x4 Treks and all I can say is Wow! This was a fantastic experience through some of the most amazing country I have ever seen, made all the better with Tony’s enthusiasm, organisational skills and 4wd knowledge. I must admit I was a bit nervous about taking a hire car into the bush but every track each day was researched, planned and executed with minimal fuss and no damage to any of the vehicles! "Cliff & I thoroughly enjoyed our first group trek. It was exciting to discover the 'outback' of NZ's South Island. Tony was passionate, knowledgable and excellent in showing us areas we would never discover on our own. Highly recommend this as a comfortable, adventurous and superb way to enjoy NZ's South Island off the beaten tourist track." - Gina Raccanello, Australia

Kimberley 4WD
Adventure Tour
15 Days

$7,800 per vehicle including 2 people
July 2024 - 2 tours

Rethink all you know about the Kimberley. We have access and experiences lined up for you on this tour that you will not experience anywhere else.

Visit the North East Kimberley frontier country for the trip of a lifetime with your experienced and accredited Savannah Guide.

Kicking off with a sunset cruise on Lake Argyle, we travel through the back roads to the Port of Wyndham. Exploring hidden gems, the Port of Wyndham and traversing the famous Karunjie Stock Route.

From Balanggarra Home Valley Station you will enjoy a laid back location and experience Balanggarra country at it’s finest. This is a great base to explore the best of El Questro.

Heading west along the Gibb River Road, via some home made scones at Ellenbrae, we make our way north on the Kalumburu Road before turning off to the Mitchell Plateau. Then we continue our adventure in and around Kalumburu to some secret swimming holes and fishing locations.

Once back on the Gibb River Road, you can either travel back to Home Valley with us (to the start of the sealed road), or you can continue towards Broome, taking in the sights of Tunnel Creek, Winjana Gorge, Derby and the Dampier Peninusla. We can supply you with information of where to go and what to see. 

You will be blown away by the diversity and culture the Kimberley Region has to offer.

There will also be opportunities for optional heli fishing, fishing charters and scenic flights.

This tour is run in association with our trusted affiliate business "See Around Corners 4WD Tours".

Duration:     14 Days
Start/End:   Lake Argyle (near Kunanarra) to Gibb/Kalumburu Rd turnoff.
Cost:  $7,800 per vehicle incl. 2 people. Extra passengers $2500

Scotland 4X4 Trek
Year round*

*Tours dependant on Covid restrictions - deposits refunded or carried over for 12 months if unable to travel.

4x4 Treks Scotland run tours throughout the year and all of our trips are fully supported by experienced guides with many years working and driving in the Highlands.

We offer tours with either hotel or wild camping overnight stays. If its a cosy bed, good meal and somewhere to relax for the evening, we have selected a series of good quality hotels along our route. Wild camp at the edge of the loch, deep within the hills, or by amazing beaches. The choice is yours.

4x4 Tours Scotland prides itself on exceeding expectation, from the length and diversity of our routes, the stunning views and vistas and the level of care we take in putting each trip together.


"The Highlands of Scotland"

Tour cost includes vehicle hire pick-up and drop-off in Edinburgh, professional tour guide instruction, mechanical assistance, all accommodation and meals. 


Duration:   7 - 10 days
Cost:   Price on application

Fishing DownUnder


https://fishingdownunder.tv/

John and Peta Haenke from Fishing DownUnder, have spent incredible amounts of time travelling around Australia whilst filming for a variety of fishing series including Wildfish. Escape with ET, and their own long running series of DVDs – Fishing DownUnder.

Specialising in sight fishing for tropical species like Golden Trevally, Permit, Queenfish, Longtail Tuna and GTs in the saltwater, as well as Barramundi in both salt and freshwater, and Saratoga and Sooty Grunter in the freshwater.

If you are looking for a bit more of an adventure, and some of the best fly fishing Queensland has to offer, I'm happy to organise road trips to a number of fishing locations chasing a range of species. You'll get to see plenty of Queensland, from the bush to the beach! I can take two anglers for a minimum of 5 days, and packages include comfortable accommodation, most meals and pickups from airport and travel to areas to be fished.

If you're not into a road trip, call me to discuss what fly fishing you'd like to experience and I'll give you the best fishing and location options.

If you'd like a trip of a lifetime to some of Queensland's less pressured, remote and isolated locations, give me a ring or email me for details.

COMING SOON IN 2024

10 Day New Zealand
High Country
Accommodated 4X4 Trek

$10,995* with your own vehicle or
$13,995* including 2 people, vehicle hire* for 12 days, plus excellent accommodation & evening meals

Experience the thrill of driving yourself through the South Island mountain country by day, while relaxing in excellent accommodation by night. Challenge yourself as you drive steep mountain tracks, ford glacial rivers, and navigate through working high country sheep stations and stunning Conservation Land.

$13,995 per vehicle - including 2 people and 4WD vehicle hire* for 12 days, or $10,995 with your own vehicle,  all dinners, excellent accommodation, vehicle insurance and roadside assistance, 10 day tour with professional tour guide instruction and backcountry mechanical assistance.

Accompanied at all times by your local guide who helps you tackle the difficult four wheel drive sections. With a maximum of 8 customer vehicles, you will feel part of the "team" in no time. 

*Hire vehicles are backed by the assurance of a reputable New Zealand owned specialty 4WD hire company. Offering a *choice of vehicles, you can upgrade from the very comfortable and capable "Standard Model" included in the cost, to a luxury "Gold Class" vehicle for an additional fee (*subject to availability). Hire vehicles are delivered to your accommodation in Christchurch on your arrival, and you drop it off at the airport in Queenstown as you leave. You can also extend the duration of your hire for an additional fee.

Duration: 10 Days on tour (12 day vehicle hire)
Start/End: Christchurch/Queenstown 
Cost: *$10,995/$13,995 per vehicle including 2 people

*Prices subject to final approval

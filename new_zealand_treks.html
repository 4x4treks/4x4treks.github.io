SOUTH ISLAND 4X4 TREKS

10 Days/11 Nights in New Zealand's South Island High Country - Accommodated 4X4 Trek

2 Tours to choose from:

• Gold Tour Package $14,995* - includes 1 or 2 people per vehicle, 10 day/11 night High Country Tour, vehicle hire for 12 days, excellent lodge/ motel/ farm stay accommodation, all meals + experiences (eg. jetboat tour, helicopter mountain flight, ski plane glacier landing (TBC)). $10,995* with your own vehicle (vehicle requirements apply).

• Silver Tour Package $7990* - includes 1 or 2 people per vehicle, 10 day/11 night High Country Tour, Vehicle Hire for 12 days, lodge/ motel/ farm stay accommodation, & all dinners. Optional extras can be booked on your behalf. $5490* with your own vehicle (vehicle requirements apply).

Experience the thrill of driving yourself through the South Island mountain country by day, while relaxing in excellent accommodation by night. Challenge yourself as you drive steep mountain tracks, ford glacial rivers, and navigate through working high country sheep stations and stunning Conservation Land.

Accompanied at all times by your qualified and accredited guide who helps you tackle the difficult four wheel drive sections. With a maximum of 8 customer vehicles, you will feel part of the "team" in no time.

*Hire vehicles are backed by the assurance of a reputable New Zealand owned specialty 4WD hire company. You drop it off at the airport in Queenstown as you leave (or back in Christchurch). You can also extend the duration of your hire for an additional fee.

Tony is an accredited Savannah Guides tour guide, EcoGuides Australia, and Tour Guides Australia certified guide, Remote Area First Aid accredited [HLTAID013 Provide First Aid in remote and isolated sites] and Cert IV Trainer & Assessor 4WD Instructor (and owner of Rockhampton 4WD Training). He has been involved in the 4WD industry for the last 18 years, guiding in Australia for 8 years since moving from New Zealand.

South Island 4X4 Treks

Accommodated High Country Adventure

This tour is "next level" as far as High Country Tag Along Tours go in New Zealand. No other operator offers such excellent accommodation, meals and experiences, making this the tour for discerning adventurers. 

Embark on an unforgettable 4WD journey from Christchurch, venturing deep into the breathtaking Southern Alps of the South Island. Revel in awe-inspiring landscapes at every turn, and discover hidden treasures often overlooked by visitors. Immerse yourself in the region's beauty with our premium accommodated 4WD tours.

Experience the thrill of driving yourself through the South Island mountain country by day, while relaxing in excellent accommodation with great meals by night. Challenge yourself as you drive steep mountain tracks, ford streams, and navigate through working high country sheep & cattle stations.

You will be accompanied at all times by your qualified guide, Tony. He's a Kiwi with an intimate knowledge of the South Island High Country's geography, geology, flora and fauna, so there's plenty to learn. As a qualified 4WD instructor, he will help you tackle the more difficult four wheel drive sections along the way. With a maximum of 5 customer vehicles, you will feel part of the "team" in no time.

The 4WD tracks have been selected for their scenic beauty rather than technical difficulty, but some of the driving will definitely get your heart racing!

It's not all about four wheel driving though, with the tour including some fantastic experiences in wonderfully scenic locations.

You will pick up your fully equipped rental 4WD vehicle (Hilux or similar ute with a lockable canopy) in Christchurch and return it to either Christchurch or Queenstown (QTN incurs an additional $870 fee). Hire vehicles are backed by the assurance of a reputable New Zealand owned hire company.

The tour includes vehicle hire for 12 days, but you can extend your rental if you want to do some extra exploring while you're there (please give us plenty of notice). It's a "roughing it in style" type of tour, so it comes with a higher price tag, but it will be well worth it! The scenery really is spectacular.

Tony has been leading remote tag along tours in Australia for the last 9 years since moving from New Zealand. He is an accredited Savannah Guide, EcoGuides Australia, and Tour Guides Australia Professional Guide, Remote Area First Aid accredited (Aust) and Cert IV Trainer & Assessor 4WD Instructor (Aust).

Before moving from NZ, he owned and operated "Overland 4WD Rentals Ltd" near Christchurch for 8 years. This business, combined with his love of the outdoors and the qualifications of a Degree in Tourism (Lincoln University, NZ) and a Masters Degree in Science specialising in Wildlife Management (Lincoln University, NZ), gave him a great knowledge of the backcountry areas of the South Island.

Arnhem Land & Kakadu
4WD Discovery Tour
21 Days

$11,500 per vehicle including 2 people

"Unprecedented Access"

This is a special tour with unprecedented access for a tag along tour.

*We have been granted special access to previously off limits areas of Arnhem Land. You are not able to individually apply for permits into some of the areas we access.

We can show you places that few people ever get to see and give you experiences that will change your view on how life is in these areas. 

You'll get to experience this almost mythical area and see the amazing scenery, wildlife and culture that makes Arnhem Land so special, all with the guidance of a professional, qualified tour guide and the safety that a group tour provides in a remote area.

Duration:     21 Days
Start/End:   Kakadu (Cooinda) to Mataranka
Cost:  $11,500 per vehicle incl. 2 people. Extra passengers $1500

Contact us for more information or to book your adventure.

WHAT ARE THE VEHICLE AND TRAILER REQUIREMENTS? 

üÖê This tour is suitable for off-road camper trailers that are set up for remote travel. Trailers with independent suspension are required as we'll be on corrugated roads for several days. üÖê A high clearance 4X4 vehicle with low range and upgraded suspension and a fuel range of 600 km is required for this tour. All terrain LT tyres (light truck construction) with over 60% tread remaining (ie. about 7mm min).

üÖê A mechanical checklist is to be completed  by a qualified mechanic at least 2 weeks prior to the start of the tour. A medical form is to be completed at least 2 weeks prior to departure also.
